Careers

Being a Swiss International Air Lines company, offers promising careers that take off your professional as well as personal life.

We are driven by our zeal for crafting exceptional experiences for our customers, and we are guided by our solid principles and values. We believe in always choosing the right path, even if it poses challenges. Being ethically correct is of a paramount importance to us. And our culture shapes a conducive environment for our employees, ensuring a meaningful and fulfilling life led at SWISStours.

If a career in moving the world excites you, we’ll be more than delighted to welcome you aboard.

You may apply to our job vacancies listed on this page or through SWISStours LinkedIn page. If there aren’t any vacancies posted, you may drop us a mail at contact@swisstours.com, detailing the job that you are looking for, an updated resume, and a covering letter. Once we have your details and a suitable position comes up, we’ll get in touch with you.

We wish you all the luck!
