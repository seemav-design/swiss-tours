    Full Day

    Visit Matterhorn, the most photographed mountain in the world. The Matterhorn glacier paradise is the highest summer ski region in Europe and is open 365 days a year.

  • Day 3
    Morning

    Travel to Grindelwald and upto Grindelwald First to experience the most exciting descent ever. Start with the First Glider or First Glider Flyer zipping through the air at speeds of up to 84 kms/hr. Then continue by your mountain cart and finally your trotti bike back to the base.

    Full Day

    Full Day

  • Day 7
    Full Day

    Visit Montreux, the beautiful town by the lake. Visit Chillon Castle, perched on a small island on Lac Lehman also known as Lake Geneva.

  • Day 8
    Full Day

    Make your way to the airport or continue your journey on your own.
